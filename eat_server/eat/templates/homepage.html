SmartCart

Helping people get healthier and wealthier, one cart at a time

 

A CS 69/169 Project...

 

Overview

SmartCart is a project for Mobile Health (CS 69/169) at Dartmouth College.

The goal of the app is to help raise awareness for healthy eating and to make it accessible to more people. A lot of the time, people are unsure what combination of nutrients they need to look for, and do not know what to buy when they enter the supermarket.

So its no longer expensive to eat healthy!
